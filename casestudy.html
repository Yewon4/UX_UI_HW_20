Problem Statement

Travelers face several challenges in the process of choosing and planning trips, including difficulties in making travel choices, lack of personalized travel information, the complexity of travel planning, time constraints, safety concerns, and budget management.

Solution

The solution is to develop an app that reduces the stress of trip planning by providing travelers with personalized travel plans tailored to their budget and preferences.

Research

User Research

As a researcher, the focus was on understanding why people utilized travel apps previously, delving into their sentiments towards these applications and the reasons behind their continued usage of specific ones. Furthermore, aimed to uncover the challenges they faced while planning their trips.
Conducted interviews with five individuals in the age group of 20 to 30.

Interview Questions

  • Do you have any specific travel goals?
  • How has your approach to travel planning changed since the pandemic began?
  • What are the biggest challenges you've encountered when planning a trip?
  • What tools or platforms do you currently use for travel planning?

Key Insight Derived

  • Prefer a travel app that consolidates flight, accommodation, and activity bookings into one platform.
  • Expressed a desire for the app to suggest options that fit within their travel budget.
  • Believe that having a pre-planned travel itinerary would alleviate the stress of trip planning.
  • Also mentioned a wish for real-time safety information during travels.

Testing & Iteration

Goals/Objectives:

  • Evaluate the usability of the login process with alternative login options.
  • Assess the app's effectiveness in guiding users through inputting their travel plans and providing relevant information.
  • Assess the general user-friendliness of the app's interface and navigation.

Feedback:

  • Has User-friendly navigation
  • Some confusion arose from the consolidation of "Confirm/book" into a single button
  • Requested a password recovery feature for users who forget their passwords
  • Curiosity about the structure of personalized questions

Conclusion

Reflecting on this project, I've come to realize that my initial enthusiasm for including a multitude of features led to a lack of clear focus. While I achieved the desired visual design, there were certain features that I didn't have the chance to implement fully.

Next steps:
  • Need to brainstorm and create a set of personalized questions to enhance the planning aspect for our users.
  • The development of the homepage interface is on the agenda to ensure a more user-friendly experience.
  • Aim to enhance the account management screen for a smoother user interaction.
  • Considering additional content for the 'Trips' screen to enrich the overall user experience.

In conclusion, this project has taught me the importance of setting clear priorities and avoiding feature overload. It also highlighted the significance of striking a balance between design and functionality. As I move forward, my focus will be on refining the user interface, introducing personalized features, and expanding the app's capabilities to ensure a more comprehensive and satisfying user experience.
